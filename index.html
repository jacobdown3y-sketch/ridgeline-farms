Our Family Farm

Quality over
quantity.

Ridgeline Farms sits high above Southern Humboldt County, overlooking majestic redwoods, rolling oak forests and the winding Eel River, with sweeping views of the world-renowned King Range.

As lifelong residents and second-generation farmers, we care deeply about cannabis — growing it with natural, organic techniques that produce the highest-quality flower while protecting the habitat we're lucky to be surrounded by. Our unique elevation creates the perfect environment for cannabis to thrive, bringing high potency and powerful terpenes to every strain we're blessed to grow.
