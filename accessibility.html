Accessibility

Last updated · July 2026

Ridgeline Farms is committed to making our website accessible to everyone, including people with disabilities.

Our approach

We aim to conform to the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 Level AA. We build with semantic HTML, keyboard-navigable controls, visible focus states, descriptive alt text, and support for reduced-motion preferences.

Ongoing effort

Accessibility is an ongoing commitment. We regularly review the site and welcome feedback that helps us improve.

Need help?

If you encounter a barrier or need content in an alternate format, contact us at info@ridgelinefarms.com and we will do our best to help.
