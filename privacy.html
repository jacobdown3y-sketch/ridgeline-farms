Privacy Policy

Last updated · July 2026

Ridgeline Farms ("we," "us," "our") respects your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect through ridgelinefarms.com and how we use it.

Information we collect

  • Information you give us — such as your name, email, phone and message when you contact us, sign up for updates, or submit a wholesale inquiry.
  • Age confirmation — we store a session flag confirming you affirmed you are 21 or older.
  • Usage data — basic, aggregated analytics such as pages visited and device type, collected via cookies.

How we use it

We use your information to respond to inquiries, send updates you request, improve the site, and comply with applicable cannabis regulations. We do not sell your personal information.

Sharing

We share information only with service providers who help us operate the site (for example, hosting and email), and where required by law.

Your choices

You can unsubscribe from emails at any time and request that we delete information you have provided by emailing info@ridgelinefarms.com.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Reach us at info@ridgelinefarms.com.
